### [My Home Lab](https://github.com/andreansx/networking-lab)

This is where I spend most of my time - exploring, building, and sometimes breaking things. It's a fully physical setup in a **36U HPC rack**, my personal space for turning ideas into reality.

*   **Router:** **MikroTik CCR2004-1G-12S+2XS**
*   **Switching:** **MikroTik CRS326-24G-2S+RM**
*   **Compute:** **Dell PowerEdge R710** (2x Intel Xeon X5670, 64GB DDR3 ECC)
*   **Daily Driver:** ThinkPad T450s running a carefully crafted **Arch Linux** environment with **i3wm**.

---

### My Approach to Learning 🌱

I see technology as a craft. For me, the most rewarding path is building a deep, practical understanding from the ground up, not just memorizing answers. Right now, my curiosity is leading me to explore:

*   Network segmentation using modern technologies like **802.1q** *(VLANs)*
*   Virtualization and contenerization with **Proxmox VE** or **FreeBSD Jails**
*   MikroTik **RouterOS**

I see certifications (like Network+, MTCNA, CCNA) as a great way to structure my learning and confirm the skills I've already developed through practice, rather than being the ultimate goal.
